## Work with a View

Fast Wi-Fi, serene surroundings, and friendly locals make Jibhi a perfect base for remote workers looking to escape crowded cities.

<img src="/assets/images/img/dubai-2.jpg" alt="Working with a mountain view" className="rounded-lg my-4" />

### Staying Connected

Most guesthouses have fiber connections now. You can also purchase local SIM cards for backup data when traveling around the valley.

### Places to Stay

- Budget homestays near the river
- Cozy guesthouses with communal spaces
- A few boutique hotels that cater to long stays

### Weekend Trips

When you need a break from the laptop, head out on a short hike or explore the nearby villages. Our [culture guide](/blog/exploring-jibhi-culture) has plenty of ideas.
